“Learn a Language in Realistic VR Scenarios with these Apps.” NSF, 8 June 2023, https://virtualspeech.com/blog/learn-language-vr-scenarios-apps.

Learning a language has been easier than ever with apps such as DuoLingo and Babbel. As technology advances so is the educational environments. This article delves into the realm of language apps in virtual reality (VR), where users are put in real-life scenarios such as ordering food or asking for directions. Through VR, users can truly experience using a language in practical situations, gaining a tangible sense of real-life language application. Traditional online language classes often incorporate real-life practice through activities such as role-playing and group discussions, however, VR language learning goes beyond by providing a more realistic and engaging learning environment.
